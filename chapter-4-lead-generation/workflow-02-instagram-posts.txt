WORKFLOW 2: Instagram Post Ideas for Listings

TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per listing
USE CASE: Creating engaging social content for new listings
ROI IMPACT: 200-400% increase in social media engagement

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a social media manager for a successful real estate agent. Generate 10 Instagram post ideas for a new listing. Include captions (150 words max), hashtag suggestions (30 hashtags), and photo/video ideas. Make the content engaging and shareable.

Property: [PROPERTY DETAILS]
Target Audience: [BUYER DEMOGRAPHIC]
Agent Personality: [PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL/LUXURY]
Local Market: [CITY/NEIGHBORHOOD]
Special Features: [UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[PROPERTY DETAILS] - Example: "4-bedroom colonial, 2,800 sq ft, $485,000"
[BUYER DEMOGRAPHIC] - Example: "Growing families"
[PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL/LUXURY] - Choose one: "Professional but approachable"
[CITY/NEIGHBORHOOD] - Example: "Westfield suburbs"
[UNIQUE SELLING POINTS] - Example: "Chef's kitchen, master suite, finished basement"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION TIPS:

For Luxury Properties:
• Use sophisticated, aspirational language
• Focus on exclusive features and lifestyle
• Emphasize quality and prestige

For First-Time Buyer Properties:
• Use encouraging, accessible language
• Highlight affordability and value
• Focus on potential and growth

For Investment Properties:
• Use data-driven language
• Highlight ROI and cash flow potential
• Focus on market trends and appreciation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Engagement rate increase: 50-100%
• Follower growth: 10-20% monthly
• Lead generation: 2-5 inquiries per listing post series
• Time savings: 1-2 hours per listing
