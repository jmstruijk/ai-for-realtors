WORKFLOW 1: Facebook Ad Copy Generator

TIME SAVED: 2-3 hours per campaign
USE CASE: Creating multiple ad variations for A/B testing
ROI IMPACT: 25-50% improvement in click-through rates

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a direct response copywriter specializing in real estate Facebook ads. Create 5 different ad copy variations for a [PROPERTY TYPE] in [NEIGHBORHOOD]. Each variation should be 125 words or less, include a strong hook, highlight key benefits, and end with a clear call-to-action. Target audience: [BUYER PERSONA]. Include emojis where appropriate.

Property Details: [INSERT DETAILS]
Unique Selling Points: [INSERT USPs]
Call-to-Action: [DESIRED ACTION]
Budget Range: [PRICE RANGE]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[PROPERTY TYPE] - Example: "3-bedroom ranch home"
[NEIGHBORHOOD] - Example: "Sunset Hills"
[BUYER PERSONA] - Example: "Young families with children"
[INSERT DETAILS] - Example: "Updated kitchen, hardwood floors, large backyard, quiet cul-de-sac"
[INSERT USPs] - Example: "Move-in ready, top-rated schools, family-friendly neighborhood"
[DESIRED ACTION] - Example: "Schedule a showing"
[PRICE RANGE] - Example: "$350,000-$375,000"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION TIPS:

For Luxury Properties:
• Use sophisticated language and exclusivity angles
• Focus on prestige, quality, and investment value
• Emphasize unique features and premium amenities

For First-Time Buyers:
• Emphasize affordability and value
• Include financing information and assistance
• Focus on starter home benefits and growth potential

For Investors:
• Highlight ROI potential and cash flow
• Include rental income estimates
• Focus on appreciation and market trends
