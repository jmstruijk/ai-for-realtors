WORKFLOW 4: LinkedIn Outreach Messages

TIME SAVED: 45 minutes per outreach campaign
USE CASE: Building referral partnerships with professionals
ROI IMPACT: 3-5 new referral partners per month

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a professional real estate agent reaching out to potential referral partners on LinkedIn. Write a connection request message and a follow-up message for [TARGET PROFESSION]. Keep it professional, value-focused, and relationship-oriented.

Target: [TARGET PROFESSION]
Your Market: [YOUR LOCATION]
Your Specialty: [YOUR NICHE]
Mutual Benefit: [HOW YOU CAN HELP EACH OTHER]
Length: Connection request 100 words, follow-up 150 words

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[TARGET PROFESSION] - Example: "Mortgage Brokers"
[YOUR LOCATION] - Example: "Denver metro area"
[YOUR NICHE] - Example: "First-time buyers"
[HOW YOU CAN HELP EACH OTHER] - Example: "Referrals for clients needing financing"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS:

For Different Professions:
• Mortgage Brokers: Focus on client financing needs
• Financial Advisors: Emphasize wealth-building through real estate
• Attorneys: Highlight estate planning and real estate law needs
• CPAs: Focus on tax implications of real estate transactions

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Connection acceptance rate: 60-80%
• Response rate: 25-40%
• Meeting conversion: 15-25%
• Referral partnerships: 3-5 per month
