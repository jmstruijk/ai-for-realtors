WORKFLOW 5: Google Ads Headlines & Descriptions

TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per campaign
USE CASE: Creating high-converting search ads
ROI IMPACT: 30-50% improvement in click-through rates

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a Google Ads specialist for real estate. Create 10 compelling headlines (30 characters max) and 5 descriptions (90 characters max) for Google search ads targeting [BUYER TYPE] in [LOCATION]. Focus on benefits, urgency, and local expertise.

Target Keywords: [PRIMARY KEYWORDS]
Buyer Type: [BUYER PERSONA]
Location: [YOUR MARKET]
Unique Selling Proposition: [WHAT MAKES YOU DIFFERENT]
Special Offers: [ANY CURRENT PROMOTIONS]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[BUYER TYPE] - Example: "First-time homebuyers"
[LOCATION] - Example: "Denver metro area"
[PRIMARY KEYWORDS] - Example: "Denver homes for sale, Denver real estate"
[BUYER PERSONA] - Example: "Young families, 30-45 years old"
[YOUR MARKET] - Example: "Denver, Colorado"
[WHAT MAKES YOU DIFFERENT] - Example: "500+ happy families, free buyer consultation"
[ANY CURRENT PROMOTIONS] - Example: "Free home valuation"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Generic headlines like "Homes For Sale" (too competitive, expensive)
❌ Exceeding character limits (ads get truncated)
❌ No clear call-to-action in descriptions
❌ Forgetting to add location extensions and call extensions

✅ Use benefit-focused headlines: "Buy Before You Sell - How?"
✅ Test multiple variations and pause low performers
✅ Include specific neighborhoods for better targeting

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Click-through rate: 3-5% (vs. 1-2% industry average)
• Cost per click: $2-4 for local real estate terms
• Conversion rate: 10-15% of clicks to consultation requests
• ROI: Track leads generated per $100 spent

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Copy 3 best headlines and 2 descriptions to Google Ads
2. Set daily budget of $20 to test
3. Add location and call extensions
4. Expected result: 5-10 clicks in first 24 hours
