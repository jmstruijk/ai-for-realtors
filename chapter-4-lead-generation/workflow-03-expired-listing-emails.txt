WORKFLOW 3: Cold Email Templates for Expired Listings

TIME SAVED: 30 minutes per email campaign
USE CASE: Converting expired listings to new clients
ROI IMPACT: 15-25% response rate vs. 2-5% industry average

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a top-producing real estate agent writing to homeowners whose listings recently expired. Write a compassionate, helpful email that offers value without being pushy. Address their likely frustrations and position yourself as the solution.

Tone: Empathetic but confident
Length: 200-250 words
Include: Market insight, different approach, soft call-to-action
Avoid: Criticizing previous agent, being overly salesy
Local Market: [YOUR MARKET]
Your Unique Approach: [WHAT MAKES YOU DIFFERENT]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[YOUR MARKET] - Example: "Westfield"
[WHAT MAKES YOU DIFFERENT] - Example: "Professional photography, strategic staging, data-driven pricing"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS:

For Different Time Frames:
• Recently expired (within 30 days): More empathetic, focus on fresh perspective
• Expired 60+ days ago: Focus on market changes and new opportunities
• Multiple expirations: Address pattern, offer comprehensive analysis

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Response rate: 15-25% vs. 2-5% industry average
• Appointment conversion: 40-60% of responses
• Listing conversion: 25-35% of appointments
• Time savings: 30 minutes per campaign
