WORKFLOW 7: Open House Promotion Content

TIME SAVED: 2-3 hours per open house
USE CASE: Creating comprehensive marketing for open houses
ROI IMPACT: 50-100% increase in open house attendance

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate marketing expert specializing in open house promotion. Create a complete marketing package for an open house with content for multiple channels. Make the content exciting and specific, highlighting unique features and creating urgency.

Property Details:
- Address: [STREET ADDRESS]
- Property Type: [SINGLE FAMILY/CONDO/TOWNHOME]
- Bedrooms/Bathrooms: [BED/BATH]
- Square Footage: [SQ FT]
- Price: [LISTING PRICE]
- Key Features: [TOP 3-5 FEATURES]
- Neighborhood: [AREA NAME]

Open House Details:
- Date: [DAY, DATE]
- Time: [START - END TIME]
- Special Features: [REFRESHMENTS, GIVEAWAYS, ETC.]

Create:
1. Facebook/Instagram post (100 words, 10 hashtags)
2. Email invitation to database (200 words)
3. Text message blast (50 words)
4. Flyer headline and description (150 words)
5. Zillow/Realtor.com description (200 words)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[STREET ADDRESS] - Example: "456 Maple Street"
[SINGLE FAMILY/CONDO/TOWNHOME] - Choose one
[BED/BATH] - Example: "4 Bed / 3 Bath"
[SQ FT] - Example: "2,400 sq ft"
[LISTING PRICE] - Example: "$485,000"
[TOP 3-5 FEATURES] - Example: "Renovated kitchen, finished basement, huge backyard"
[AREA NAME] - Example: "Sunset Hills"
[DAY, DATE] - Example: "Sunday, October 27th"
[START - END TIME] - Example: "1:00 PM - 3:00 PM"
[REFRESHMENTS, GIVEAWAYS, ETC.] - Example: "Fresh cookies, coffee, gift baskets for first 10 visitors"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS:

For Different Property Types:
• Luxury homes: Emphasize exclusivity, "private showing available," high-end finishes
• Starter homes: Focus on affordability, first-time buyer friendly, low maintenance
• Investment properties: Highlight rental potential, cash flow, appreciation history
• Fixer-uppers: Emphasize potential, "bring your vision," great bones

For Different Seasons:
• Spring/Summer: Highlight outdoor spaces, natural light, gardens
• Fall: Cozy features, fireplaces, holiday entertaining potential
• Winter: Energy efficiency, insulation, warm and inviting spaces

For Different Neighborhoods:
• Family areas: Schools, parks, safety, community events
• Urban: Walkability, restaurants, nightlife, public transit
• Suburban: Space, quiet, good schools, family-friendly
• Rural: Privacy, land, nature, peaceful setting

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Posting only once the day before (need 5-7 days advance notice)
❌ Generic "Open House Sunday" with no details or excitement
❌ No call-to-action or RSVP mechanism
❌ Forgetting to include address or time clearly
❌ Not promoting across multiple channels

✅ Start promoting 7 days in advance
✅ Post daily reminders on social media
✅ Include specific features that create excitement
✅ Add incentives (refreshments, giveaways) to increase attendance
✅ Make it easy to RSVP or ask questions

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Attendance: Target 15-25 visitors (vs. 5-10 without promotion)
• Engagement rate: 5-10% of social media followers attend
• Email open rate: 25-35% for database invitations
• Conversion: 10-20% of attendees request follow-up showings
