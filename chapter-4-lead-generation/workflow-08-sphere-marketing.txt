WORKFLOW 8: Sphere Marketing Messages

TIME SAVED: 3-4 hours per month
USE CASE: Staying top-of-mind with personal network
ROI IMPACT: 40-60% of business from sphere of influence

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate agent with a strong sphere of influence. Create 12 monthly sphere marketing messages (one per month) that keep you top-of-mind without being salesy. Each message should provide value, feel personal, and include a soft call-to-action related to real estate.

Message types to include:
- Market updates (2)
- Helpful tips (3)
- Personal updates (2)
- Community information (2)
- Seasonal content (2)
- Holiday greetings (1)

Your Market: [YOUR CITY/AREA]
Your Personality: [PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL/HUMOROUS]
Your Niche: [IF ANY - e.g., first-time buyers, luxury, etc.]
Tone: Friendly and helpful, not salesy

Format each as: Email subject line + 150-word message + clear call-to-action

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[YOUR CITY/AREA] - Example: "Denver metro area"
[PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL/HUMOROUS] - Choose one that fits your style
[IF ANY] - Example: "First-time buyers" or leave blank if general

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Only reaching out when you need something
❌ Generic mass emails that feel impersonal
❌ Too frequent (monthly is plenty)
❌ Always talking about real estate
❌ No clear call-to-action

✅ Provide value first (tips, information, community)
✅ Mix personal and professional content
✅ Make it easy to respond or refer
✅ Be consistent (same day each month)
✅ Personalize with names and specific details when possible

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Open rate: Target 40-50% (higher than typical marketing)
• Response rate: 5-10% of recipients reply
• Referrals generated: 2-3 per month from sphere
• Transactions: 40-60% of business from sphere annually

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Choose 3 messages (January, February, March)
2. Customize with your market and personality
3. Schedule in your email system
4. Expected result: 2-3 responses and 1 referral in 90 days
