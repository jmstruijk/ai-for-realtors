WORKFLOW 10: Niche Market Targeting Messages

TIME SAVED: 4-5 hours per niche campaign
USE CASE: Targeting specific buyer/seller segments
ROI IMPACT: 50-100% higher conversion than generic marketing

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate agent specializing in [NICHE]. Create a complete marketing package targeting this specific audience. Show deep understanding of their unique needs, fears, and motivations. Make them feel like you "get" their situation.

Target Niche: [CHOOSE ONE]
- First-time home buyers
- Luxury home buyers/sellers
- Real estate investors
- Downsizers/empty nesters
- Military/VA buyers
- Divorce situations
- Relocating professionals
- Multi-generational buyers

Create:
1. Facebook ad copy (100 words)
2. Email to targeted list (250 words)
3. Landing page headline + description (200 words)
4. Social media post series (3 posts)
5. Unique value proposition statement

Your Market: [YOUR AREA]
Your Experience: [RELEVANT CREDENTIALS]
Tone: Empathetic and expert, not salesy

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[NICHE] - Choose from the list above
[YOUR AREA] - Example: "Austin metro area"
[RELEVANT CREDENTIALS] - Example: "Helped 50+ first-time buyers in past 2 years"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Generic advice that doesn't consider specific situation
❌ Not understanding the niche's unique pain points
❌ Using same messaging across all niches
❌ Claiming expertise without credentials

✅ Deep dive into niche-specific needs
✅ Show genuine understanding and empathy
✅ Customize everything for the niche
✅ Build credibility with relevant experience

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Conversion rate: 50-100% higher than generic marketing
• Lead quality: 80% of niche leads are qualified
• Close rate: 40-60% higher with niche positioning
• Referrals: Niche clients refer similar clients
