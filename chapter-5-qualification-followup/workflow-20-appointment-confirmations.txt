WORKFLOW 20: Appointment Confirmation & Reminder Sequences

TIME SAVED: 1 hour per week
USE CASE: Reducing no-shows and last-minute cancellations
ROI IMPACT: 40-60% reduction in no-shows

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate agent who rarely experiences no-shows due to excellent appointment management. Create confirmation and reminder sequences for different appointment types:

1. Buyer showing appointment
2. Seller consultation
3. Listing presentation
4. Open house commitment
5. Closing appointment

For each, create:
- Immediate confirmation (within 5 minutes of scheduling)
- 24-hour reminder
- 2-hour reminder
- Day-of logistics message

Include both email and text versions.
Make messages helpful and excitement-building, not just transactional.

Your Name: [YOUR NAME]
Your Phone: [YOUR PHONE]
Your Style: [PROFESSIONAL/FRIENDLY]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[YOUR NAME] - Your first name
[YOUR PHONE] - Your phone number
[PROFESSIONAL/FRIENDLY] - Choose your communication style

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Only sending one reminder (or none)
❌ Reminders that are too formal/transactional
❌ Not confirming 24 hours before
❌ Not providing clear logistics

✅ Send multiple reminders at strategic times
✅ Make reminders helpful and friendly
✅ Always get confirmation 24 hours before
✅ Provide specific meeting details

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• No-show rate: 5-10% (vs. 20-30% without reminders)
• Reschedule rate: 15-20% (better than no-show)
• Client satisfaction: 85%+ appreciate the reminders
• Time saved: 5-8 hours per month (fewer wasted trips)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Set up text templates for 24-hour and 2-hour reminders
2. Set phone reminders to send them
3. Use for next 5 appointments
4. Expected result: Zero no-shows
