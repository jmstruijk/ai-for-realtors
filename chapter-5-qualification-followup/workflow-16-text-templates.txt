WORKFLOW 16: Text Message Templates

TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per week
USE CASE: Quick, effective text communication with leads and clients
ROI IMPACT: 70-80% response rate vs. 25-35% for emails

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate agent who communicates effectively via text. Create 15 text message templates for common situations. Each must be:
- Short (under 160 characters when possible, max 300)
- Clear and actionable
- Easy to respond to (yes/no or simple answer)
- Professional but casual
- Include emoji where appropriate (but not excessive)

Situations:
1. Initial contact (online lead)
2. Appointment confirmation
3. Running late notification
4. Post-showing follow-up
5. New listing alert
6. Price drop notification
7. Offer update
8. Inspection scheduled
9. Closing reminder
10. Thank you after closing
11. Referral request
12. Check-in with past client
13. Open house reminder
14. Document request
15. Quick question

Your Name: [YOUR NAME]
Your Style: [PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[YOUR NAME] - Your first name
[PROFESSIONAL/CASUAL] - Choose your communication style

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Response rate: 70-80% (vs. 25-35% for email)
• Response time: Average 15-30 minutes
• Read rate: 98% (texts almost always read)
• Preferred method: 60% of clients prefer text over call/email
