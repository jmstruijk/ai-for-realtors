WORKFLOW 44: Email Newsletter Templates

TIME SAVED: 2-3 hours per newsletter
USE CASE: Monthly communication with database
ROI IMPACT: 20-30% of business from newsletter subscribers

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate marketing expert. Create 12 monthly email newsletter templates (300-400 words each) for real estate agents. Each should provide value, include market update, and have clear call-to-action.

Monthly themes:
January: New year market predictions
February: Winter home maintenance
March: Spring selling season preview
April: Buyer's market update
May: Summer home prep
June: Vacation home security
July: Mid-year market review
August: Back-to-school neighborhoods
September: Fall maintenance checklist
October: Year-end tax planning
November: Holiday home safety
December: Year in review + gratitude

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Only sending when you need something
❌ Too salesy (no value provided)
❌ Inconsistent sending schedule

✅ Send monthly on same day
✅ Provide value first, sell second
✅ Keep it conversational

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Open rate: 35-45% (vs. 20-25% industry average)
• Click rate: 8-12%
• Referrals: 20-30% of business from subscribers
• Engagement: 5-10% reply to newsletters

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Use next month's template
2. Customize with local data
3. Send to database
4. Expected result: 3-5 responses, 1-2 leads
