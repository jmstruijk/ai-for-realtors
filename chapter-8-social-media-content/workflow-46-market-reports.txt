WORKFLOW 46: Market Report Summaries

TIME SAVED: 2 hours per market report
USE CASE: Creating monthly market reports
ROI IMPACT: Establish authority, generate leads

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a market analyst who makes data interesting. Create monthly market report template that transforms statistics into engaging insights.

Input data:
- Median sale price (current vs. last month vs. last year)
- Average days on market
- Inventory levels
- Absorption rate
- List-to-sale price ratio
- Number of sales

Create report with:
1. Executive Summary (3-5 key takeaways)
2. Pricing Trends
3. Inventory Analysis
4. Market Predictions
5. Buyer Implications
6. Seller Implications
7. Visual elements

Tone: Authoritative but accessible
Length: 400-500 words

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Just presenting data without insights
❌ Too technical/jargon-heavy
❌ No actionable takeaways

✅ Explain what data means
✅ Use plain English
✅ Provide clear implications

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Lead generation: 10-15 leads per month from report
• SEO ranking: Top 10 for target keywords
• Perceived expertise: Recognized as market expert
• Sharing: 20-30% share with others

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Create report from MLS data
2. Send to database
3. Expected result: 5-10 consultation requests
