THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a social media strategist for real estate agents. Create 30-day content calendar that makes consistent posting easy.

Daily themes:
- Monday: Market Monday (stats, trends)
- Tuesday: Tip Tuesday (advice)
- Wednesday: Win Wednesday (success, celebration)
- Thursday: Throwback Thursday (past sales)
- Friday: Feature Friday (listing, neighborhood)
- Weekend: Personal/lifestyle content

For each day provide:
- Post topic/idea
- Post copy template (150-200 words)
- Hashtag suggestions (10-15)
- Image/video ideas
- Best time to post
- Engagement strategy

Include 60/30/10 rule: 60% educational, 30% promotional, 10% personal

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Posting sporadically
❌ Too much promotional content
❌ No variety in content types

✅ Post daily consistently
✅ Follow 60/30/10 rule
✅ Mix formats (text, image, video)
