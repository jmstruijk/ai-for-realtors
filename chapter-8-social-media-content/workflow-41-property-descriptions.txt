WORKFLOW 41: Property Description Generator

TIME SAVED: 45 minutes per listing
USE CASE: Compelling MLS and marketing descriptions
ROI IMPACT: 200-300% more inquiries on listings

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a professional real estate copywriter known for descriptions that sell homes fast. Write a compelling property description for MLS and marketing materials. Use descriptive language that helps buyers visualize living in the home. Highlight unique features and lifestyle benefits, not just features.

Property Details: [INSERT DETAILS]
Target Buyer: [BUYER PERSONA]
Unique Features: [KEY SELLING POINTS]
Neighborhood: [AREA HIGHLIGHTS]
Price: [LISTING PRICE]
Length: 200-250 words
Tone: Engaging and aspirational
Include: Lifestyle benefits, emotional triggers, call-to-action

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[INSERT DETAILS] - Example: "4BR/3BA colonial, 2,400 sq ft, updated kitchen"
[BUYER PERSONA] - Example: "Growing families with children"
[KEY SELLING POINTS] - Example: "Updated kitchen, finished basement, large backyard"
[AREA HIGHLIGHTS] - Example: "Top-rated schools, quiet street, parks nearby"
[LISTING PRICE] - Example: "$485,000"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VARIATIONS FOR DIFFERENT PROPERTY TYPES:

For Luxury Properties:
You are a luxury real estate copywriter. Write an elegant, sophisticated property description that appeals to affluent buyers. Focus on exclusivity, quality, and prestige. Use refined language and emphasize investment value.

For Condos/Townhomes:
You are writing for busy professionals who value convenience and low maintenance. Emphasize location, amenities, and lifestyle benefits. Focus on urban conveniences and community features.

For Investment Properties:
You are writing for real estate investors. Focus on ROI potential, rental income, appreciation prospects, and practical investment benefits. Include relevant market data and investment highlights.

• Inquiry rate: 200-300% increase vs. generic descriptions
• Showing requests: 150-250% more than comparable listings
• Days on market: 20-30% faster sales
• Agent branding: Professional, consistent marketing voice
