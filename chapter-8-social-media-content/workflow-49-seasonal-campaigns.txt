WORKFLOW 49: Seasonal Marketing Campaign Templates

TIME SAVED: 3-4 hours per campaign
USE CASE: Running seasonal promotions
ROI IMPACT: Seasonal campaigns generate 50% more leads

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a marketing strategist creating seasonal real estate campaigns. Create 4 complete campaign templates (one per season) with all marketing assets.

For each season create:
- Campaign theme and messaging
- Email sequence (3 emails)
- Social media posts (10 posts)
- Facebook/Instagram ads (3 variations)
- Landing page copy
- Call-to-action and offer

Seasons:
- Spring: "Spring into Your New Home"
- Summer: "Summer Selling Success"
- Fall: "Fall in Love with Your New Home"
- Winter: "New Year, New Home"

Include timeline and implementation checklist.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Generic campaigns not tied to season
❌ No clear timeline or plan
❌ Missing key marketing assets

✅ Leverage seasonal psychology
✅ Plan and schedule in advance
✅ Create all assets upfront
