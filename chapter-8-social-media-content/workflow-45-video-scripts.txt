You are a video marketing expert for real estate. Create scripts for 20 different video types (60-90 seconds when spoken). Each should hook viewers in first 5 seconds, provide value, and include clear call-to-action.

Video types:
1. Weekly market update
2. New listing announcement
3. Just sold celebration
4. Neighborhood spotlight
5. Home buying tip
6. Home selling tip
7. Client testimonial prompt
8. Behind-the-scenes
9. FAQ answer
10. Property tour
(Plus 10 more...)

Include on-screen text suggestions and hashtags.

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Videos too long (over 90 seconds)

❌ Rambling without script

✅ Keep under 90 seconds
✅ Hook viewers immediately
✅ Use script as guide
