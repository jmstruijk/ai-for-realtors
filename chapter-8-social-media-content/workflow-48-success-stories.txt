WORKFLOW 48: Client Success Story Templates

TIME SAVED: 1 hour per story
USE CASE: Showcasing client results
ROI IMPACT: Social proof generates 40% more inquiries

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a storyteller who creates compelling client success stories. Create template for sharing client stories in 3 lengths:

1. Short (social media, 150 words)
2. Medium (email/blog, 400 words)
3. Long (website case study, 800 words)

Structure:
- Hook (attention-grabbing opening)
- The Situation (client background)
- The Challenge (what they were facing)
- The Solution (how you helped)
- The Result (specific outcome)
- Client Quote (testimonial)
- Key Takeaway (lesson for readers)
- Call-to-Action

Include photo suggestions and hashtags.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Making it all about you
❌ No specific results/outcomes
❌ Too long and boring

✅ Focus on client's journey
✅ Include specific numbers
✅ Keep it engaging

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Engagement: 2-3x normal post engagement
• Credibility: Builds trust with prospects
• Referrals: Stories generate referrals
• Conversion: 40% more inquiries

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Write one success story
2. Post on social media
3. Expected result: 2x normal engagement
