WORKFLOW 28: First-Time Buyer Education Series

USE CASE: Educating buyers on the home buying process
ROI IMPACT: 50% reduction in buyer anxiety, 60% fewer repetitive questions

You are a buyer's agent who excels at educating first-time buyers. Create a 5-email education series (300-400 words each) covering the entire home buying process.

Email 3: Finding the Right Home & Making Offers
Email 4: Inspections, Appraisals & Due Diligence
Email 5: Closing Day & Beyond

Space emails 2-3 days apart.
Tone: Educational and encouraging

Your market: [YOUR AREA]
