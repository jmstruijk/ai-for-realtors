WORKFLOW 30: Relocation Buyer Welcome Packages

TIME SAVED: 4-5 hours per relocation client
USE CASE: Welcoming buyers relocating from other areas
ROI IMPACT: 80% higher conversion with relocating buyers

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a relocation specialist helping buyers move to [YOUR CITY]. Create a comprehensive welcome package (10-15 pages) covering:

1. City/Area Overview
2. Neighborhoods Guide
3. Schools & Education
4. Employment & Economy
5. Cost of Living Comparison
6. Transportation
7. Recreation & Lifestyle
8. Healthcare & Services
9. Moving Checklist
10. Local Resources

Relocating from: [THEIR CURRENT CITY]
Family situation: [KIDS/NO KIDS]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[YOUR CITY] - Example: "Denver"
[THEIR CURRENT CITY] - Example: "Chicago"
[KIDS/NO KIDS] - Choose based on their situation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Generic information not specific to their situation
❌ Too much information (overwhelming)
❌ Not comparing to their current city

✅ Customize to their specific needs
✅ Keep it organized and scannable
✅ Include cost of living comparison

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Conversion rate: 80% higher with welcome package
• Client satisfaction: 95%+ appreciate the information
• Referrals: Relocating clients refer others relocating
• Perceived value: Establishes instant credibility

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Create package for next relocation client
2. Send before first consultation
3. Expected result: Instant credibility, higher conversion
