WORKFLOW 25: Home Search Criteria Questionnaire

TIME SAVED: 2-3 hours per buyer
USE CASE: Defining precise buyer needs before showing homes
ROI IMPACT: 50% fewer unnecessary showings, 40% faster to find "the one"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a buyer's agent who excels at understanding client needs. Create a comprehensive home search criteria questionnaire with 25-30 questions organized into categories. For each question, provide why you're asking it and how to use the answer.

Categories:
1. Basic Requirements (5 questions)
2. Location & Lifestyle (8 questions)
3. Property Features (7 questions)
4. Financial Considerations (5 questions)
5. Timeline & Motivation (5 questions)

Market: [YOUR AREA]
Buyer type: [FIRST-TIME/MOVE-UP/LUXURY/INVESTOR]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[YOUR AREA] - Example: "Denver metro area"
[FIRST-TIME/MOVE-UP/LUXURY/INVESTOR] - Choose buyer type

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Asking all questions in one sitting (overwhelming)
❌ Not explaining why you're asking each question
❌ Ignoring answers and showing wrong properties

✅ Break questionnaire into 2-3 conversations
✅ Explain the purpose of each section
✅ Actually use the answers to filter properties

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Showing efficiency: 50% reduction in unnecessary showings
• Time to find home: 40% faster (6 weeks vs. 10 weeks average)
• Buyer satisfaction: 90%+ feel understood
• Offer confidence: 85% make offer on first or second favorite

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Send first 10 questions to next buyer
2. Review answers before scheduling showings
3. Show only homes matching top 5 priorities
4. Expected result: 50% fewer showings, happier buyer
