TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per consultation
USE CASE: First meeting with potential buyers
ROI IMPACT: 60% higher buyer commitment rate

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a top buyer's agent with high consultation-to-commitment conversion. Create a 45-60 minute consultation script that flows naturally and ends with clear commitment.

Structure:
1. Opening & Rapport Building (5 min)
2. Understanding Their Situation (15 min)
3. Explaining Your Process (10 min)
4. Demonstrating Your Value (10 min)
5. Addressing Concerns (5 min)
6. Closing for Commitment (5 min)

Your market: [YOUR AREA]
Your experience: [YEARS/CREDENTIALS]

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Rushing to the agreement without building rapport
❌ Not explaining the value of exclusive representation
❌ Being pushy or desperate

✅ Build genuine connection first
✅ Clearly articulate your value
✅ Ask for the commitment directly
