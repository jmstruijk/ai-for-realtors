WORKFLOW 26: Post-Showing Feedback Forms

TIME SAVED: 30 minutes per showing day
USE CASE: Capturing buyer reactions immediately after showings
ROI IMPACT: 40% faster to identify "the one"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a buyer's agent who captures showing feedback effectively. Create a post-showing feedback form that can be completed in 2-3 minutes via text or email.

Include:
- Overall rating (1-10 scale)
- Specific feature ratings
- What they loved / didn't love
- Comparison to previous favorites
- "Would you make an offer?" question

Create two versions:
1. Quick version (5 questions, 2 minutes) - for text
2. Detailed version (10 questions, 5 minutes) - for email

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Waiting until end of day to ask for feedback
❌ Making form too long (buyers won't complete it)
❌ Not actually using the feedback

✅ Send immediately after each showing
✅ Keep it short and mobile-friendly
✅ Review feedback before next showing

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Response rate: 80-90% complete feedback forms
• Time to decision: 40% faster to identify top choice
• Showing efficiency: 30% fewer repeat viewings needed
• Offer confidence: Buyers feel 85% more confident

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Save quick feedback template in your phone
2. Text it to buyers after next showing
3. Review responses before showing more homes
4. Expected result: Clearer picture of what they want
