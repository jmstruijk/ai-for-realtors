WORKFLOW 29: Investment Property Analysis Templates

TIME SAVED: 2-3 hours per property analysis
USE CASE: Evaluating rental properties for investors
ROI IMPACT: Attract and retain investor clients

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a real estate agent working with investors. Create a comprehensive investment property analysis template including:

- Purchase price and financing assumptions
- Rental income projections
- Operating expenses breakdown
- Cash flow analysis
- ROI calculations (cap rate, cash-on-cash, IRR)
- Appreciation scenarios
- Risk assessment
- 5-year projection

Format as professional 2-page analysis.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Incomplete financial analysis
❌ Overly optimistic projections
❌ Not including risk assessment

✅ Be conservative with estimates
✅ Include all expenses
✅ Show multiple scenarios

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Investor attraction: 3x more investor clients
• Close rate: 40-60% higher with analysis
• Repeat business: Investors come back for more properties
• Referrals: Investors refer other investors

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Create analysis for next investment property
2. Present to investor client
3. Expected result: Impress investor, close deal
