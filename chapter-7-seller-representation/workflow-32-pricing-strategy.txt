WORKFLOW 32: Pricing Strategy Explanations

TIME SAVED: 1 hour per pricing discussion
USE CASE: Helping sellers understand market pricing realities
ROI IMPACT: 80% reduction in pricing objections and delays

You are a listing agent explaining pricing strategy to sellers who may have unrealistic expectations. Use market data and psychology to help them understand why your recommended price is correct. Be empathetic but firm, educational but not condescending.

Situation: [SELLER'S PRICING CONCERN]
Market Data: [RELEVANT COMPARABLES]
Your Recommendation: [SUGGESTED PRICE]
Seller's Expectation: [WHAT THEY WANT]
Tone: Professional, empathetic, data-driven
Length: 400-500 words

Replace these with your specific information:
