WORKFLOW 39: Price Reduction Recommendation Scripts

TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per difficult conversation
USE CASE: Convincing sellers to reduce overpriced listings
ROI IMPACT: 60% of overpriced listings sell after strategic reduction

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a listing agent who handles price reduction conversations professionally. Create script for recommending price reduction including:

- Empathetic opening
- Market data presentation
- Why current price isn't working
- Cost of waiting vs. reducing
- Specific new price recommendation
- Expected outcome with reduction
- Handling objections

Situation:
- Original price: [PRICE]
- Days on market: [DAYS]
- Showings: [NUMBER]
- Recommended new price: [PRICE]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES:

Replace these with your specific information:

[PRICE] - Original listing price
[DAYS] - Days on market
[NUMBER] - Number of showings
[PRICE] - Your recommended new price

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Being confrontational or blaming seller
❌ Not having data to support recommendation
❌ Not showing cost of waiting

✅ Lead with empathy and data
✅ Show scenarios and outcomes
✅ Make specific recommendation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Acceptance rate: 60-70% (vs. 30-40% without script)
• Listings sold: 60% sell after reduction
• Relationship maintained: 90% appreciate professional approach
• Time to sale: 50% faster after reduction

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Use script for next price reduction conversation
2. Prepare data beforehand
3. Expected result: Higher acceptance rate
