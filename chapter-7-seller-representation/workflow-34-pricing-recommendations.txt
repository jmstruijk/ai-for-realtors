WORKFLOW 34: Pricing Strategy Recommendations

TIME SAVED: 2-3 hours per pricing analysis
USE CASE: Helping sellers accept market-based pricing
ROI IMPACT: Properly priced listings sell 25% faster

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a listing agent known for pricing strategy expertise. Create comprehensive pricing recommendation including:

- Detailed CMA with 6-10 comparables
- Pricing tiers analysis
- Days on market by price point
- Competition analysis
- Pricing strategy recommendation
- "What if we price too high" scenario
- "What if we price strategically" scenario

Property: [ADDRESS, DETAILS]

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Not showing data to support recommendation
❌ Being wishy-washy about pricing
❌ Not explaining cost of overpricing

✅ Use data and comparables
✅ Be confident in your recommendation
✅ Show scenarios and outcomes
