WORKFLOW 36: Pre-Listing Seller Questionnaire

TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per listing
USE CASE: Gathering all necessary information before listing
ROI IMPACT: 50% faster to market, fewer delays

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a listing agent who is extremely organized. Create comprehensive pre-listing questionnaire gathering all information needed to list a property.

Include sections for:
- Property Details
- Features & Upgrades
- Inclusions & Exclusions
- Disclosure Items
- Showing Preferences
- Pricing Expectations
- Motivation & Timeline
- Special Circumstances
- HOA Information
- Utilities & Services

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Questionnaire too long (won't complete)
❌ Not explaining why you need each item
❌ Not following up on incomplete answers

✅ Keep it organized and clear
✅ Explain purpose of each section
✅ Review together if needed

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Time to list: 50% faster
• Missing information: 90% reduction
• Listing accuracy: 99%+ accurate
• Seller satisfaction: Appreciate organization

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Send questionnaire before listing appointment
2. Review answers together
3. Expected result: Arrive fully prepared
