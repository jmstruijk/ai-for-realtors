WORKFLOW 38: Seller Communication Templates

TIME SAVED: 1-2 hours per week per listing
USE CASE: Keeping sellers informed throughout listing period
ROI IMPACT: 90% seller satisfaction, fewer anxious calls

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are a listing agent who excels at seller communication. Create weekly update email template for active listings including:

- Showing Activity Summary
- Buyer Feedback Compilation
- Marketing Metrics
- Market Update
- Recommendations
- Next Week's Plan
- Questions Answered

Tone: Professional, reassuring, proactive
Length: 300-400 words

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Inconsistent communication
❌ Only contacting when there's bad news
❌ Generic updates with no specifics

✅ Send weekly updates consistently
✅ Include both good and challenging news
✅ Provide specific data and recommendations

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Seller satisfaction: 90%+ (vs. 60-70% without updates)
• Anxious calls: 70% reduction
• Trust level: Significantly higher
• Referrals: More likely to refer

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Send weekly update to all active listings
2. Include specific data
3. Expected result: Happier sellers, fewer calls
