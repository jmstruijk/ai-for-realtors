WORKFLOW 40: Open House Host Scripts

TIME SAVED: 30 minutes per open house
USE CASE: Maximizing lead generation at open houses
ROI IMPACT: 40% more qualified leads per open house

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE COMPLETE PROMPT:

You are an open house expert who converts 60% of visitors to leads. Create conversation scripts for 6 visitor types:

1. Serious buyers
2. Curious neighbors
3. "Just browsing"
4. Potential sellers
5. Investors
6. Difficult visitors

For each include:
- Greeting (first 30 seconds)
- Qualification questions
- Feature highlighting
- Contact capture
- Follow-up commitment

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:

❌ Being too pushy or salesy
❌ Not qualifying visitors
❌ Letting people leave without contact info

✅ Be friendly and helpful first
✅ Ask qualifying questions naturally
✅ Always capture contact information

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SUCCESS METRICS:

• Lead capture: 40-60% of visitors (vs. 20-30% without scripts)
• Quality leads: 70% are qualified
• Follow-up rate: 80% agree to follow-up
• Conversions: 10-15% eventually transact

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUICK WIN (5 minutes):
1. Use scripts at next open house
2. Practice greetings beforehand
3. Expected result: 2x lead capture rate
